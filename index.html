Data Infrastructure for Experiment Support

This website is a repository of relevant datasets for the projects to be selected in the 1st Open Call of Mediafutures, an EU-funded virtual European data innovation hub to support entrepreneurial and innovative projects to reshape the media value chain.

This repository also includes lists of technological tools recommended by Mediafutures mentors for startups and artists to collect new data, analyze and visualize datasets in order to produce novel products, services and interventions on data, technology and arts.
